About Colgra

Welcome to Colgra Clothing. #blackowned #madeinafrica #madeinghana

Founded in 2019 by Samuel Andy Kellson, Colgra Clothing is an online African fashion store that caters to fashionable Afrocentric unisex clothing.

We are a brand that transforms and creates high-quality modern and trendy looks using African textiles and wax prints.

We take pride in each of our pieces being hand made in our production studio in West Africa Ghana with rich fabrics sourced from different parts of Africa.

Colgra Clothing is a celebration of African culture, but not just for those with African heritage. It is a way to be bold, be proud, and express yourself, whoever you are.
